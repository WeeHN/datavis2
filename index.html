The Cost of Living Crisis in Australia

Figure 1: Worldwide Cost of Living Index (2022)

The rising cost of living is driven by inflation, increased housing and healthcare costs, and higher education, energy, and food prices. This poses challenges in finding affordable housing, managing healthcare and education expenses, and maintaining living standards. Income stagnation worsens these issues, leading to income inequality, reduced access to essential services, and increased poverty risks. Addressing this requires balancing government policies for economic growth, price stability, and social equity with individual financial adaptation to changing economic conditions.
